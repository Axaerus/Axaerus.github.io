01Neighbourhood Nash

University Project

2022

itch.io Page

Credits & Crew

  • Olav J. Digranes

"Start your engines!"

Neighbourhood Nash is a small game developed for the Games Engineering module of my university course. Olav and I were paired together and tasked with the development of a game for as a final graded asset, where the game's engine would be built from the ground up (a first for myself and Olav). The idea surrounding the game grew out of jokes between myself and Olav combining silly, light-hearted scenarios into a cohesive and structured game to build upon. The game itself is written in C++, utilising the Box2D and SFML libraries.

It should be noted that the game is still slated for further development, with the development postponed indefinitely due to current academic work.
