Rory Gatens

Hi, I'm Rory! I'm currently a student at Edinburgh Napier University studying BSc (Hons) Game Development. My pursuit into Games Development comes from a life-long passion for games, starting all the way back with Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 4 on the PS2. I have since worked in the games industry as part of the Quality Assurance team with 4J Studios, spending the 4 years between 2016 and 2020 assisting the delivery of Minecraft: Console Edition updates and marketplace content. Since then, my experience has extended to Software Intern positions at 4J Studios to continue as part of the development team to create and assist in internal projects.
